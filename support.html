Common questions

When does Praxis launch?
Praxis is launching on iOS. Join the waitlist on the home page and we'll email you the day it goes live.
How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are billed through your Apple ID. You can view, change, or cancel anytime in iOS Settings → your name → Subscriptions. Cancelling stops future renewals; access continues until the end of the current period.
Is Praxis medical advice?
No. Praxis is an educational protocol library, not medical advice. Talk to a qualified healthcare professional before making significant changes to your health routine.
How do I delete my account and data?
Email genti@runpraxis.app from your account address and we'll permanently delete your account and associated data.
